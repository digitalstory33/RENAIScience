RENAISSANCE

In this project we have selected art historians who have showed interest in the Renaissance in their collection which was indexed in the Artchive. The Artchive contains data about art historian such as Italian, European, American, as well as data from some of the archival collections that they created.These collections contain information on what art historians have studied over the course of their lives (research notes, photographs, correspondences) allowing us to understand, which historical periods, subject or specific aspect of the Renaissance was studied
